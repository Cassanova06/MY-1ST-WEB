The Problem

Every day, millions of Tanzanians make decisions without knowing where the best prices, quality, or service really are. Information is scattered across different apps, websites, and word-of-mouth — time-consuming, frustrating, and it often leads to overpaying or choosing the wrong provider.

The Big Idea

What if you could see all your options in one place — price, quality, popularity, and real customer reviews — before you decide? Linganisha App does exactly that. In seconds, compare multiple providers and choose the one that truly works for you.

How It Works

Four steps from question to decision.

2

Instantly see price, rating, users, and reviews side by side

The Business Model

We generate revenue through five complementary streams.

  • Sponsored listings for service providers
  • Verification badges for trusted vendors
  • In-app ads (non-intrusive)
  • Subscriptions for daily price alerts (e.g., fuel drops, data discounts)
  • Referral commissions for clicks and sign-ups

Executive Summary

Linganisha App is a mobile-first price and service comparison platform for Tanzania. Every day, millions of people choose mobile money services, internet bundles, transport, fuel, and other everyday services without a clear, unified view of price, quality, and real user feedback — leading to overpaying and poor choices.

Linganisha solves this by letting users pick a category, enter an amount or product, and instantly compare providers on price, star rating, user base size, and reviews — all in Swahili, on mobile, in seconds. Launching with 20+ categories spanning money transfer, connectivity, transport, real estate, agriculture, jobs, fuel, education, health, and home appliances, the app is built to become the default decision-making layer for everyday spending in Tanzania.

Revenue comes from five streams: sponsored listings, vendor verification badges, non-intrusive in-app ads, price-alert subscriptions, and referral commissions — aligning incentives so providers pay to be discovered while comparisons stay transparent for users. With rising smartphone and internet penetration across Tanzania, Linganisha is positioned to expand across East Africa and become the region's trusted price comparison platform.
